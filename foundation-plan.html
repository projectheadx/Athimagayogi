Our Sacred Mission

To awaken the inner light (Aga Oli) in every soul through the ancient Siddhar wisdom of Vasi Yoga, making the profound science of breath and consciousness accessible to all humanity, regardless of background, religion, or circumstance.

Our Strategic Pillars

Five core areas that form the foundation of our spiritual mission and organizational growth

01

Spiritual Education

Systematic teaching of Siddhar wisdom through structured programs, workshops, and courses for all levels of seekers.

  • Foundation courses
  • Advanced programs
  • Online learning platform
  • Certification programs
02

Community Building

Creating a global community of like-minded seekers who support each other on the spiritual path.

  • Local satsang groups
  • Online community forums
  • Annual gatherings
  • Volunteer programs
03

Service & Outreach

Extending the spirit of love and compassion through charitable activities and social service.

  • Annadhanam (Food donation)
  • Free classes for needy
  • Community health camps
  • Rural outreach programs
04

Preservation of Wisdom

Documenting and preserving the ancient Siddhar teachings for future generations.

  • Digital library
  • Video archive
  • Written publications
  • Translation projects
05

Global Expansion

Sharing the Siddhar wisdom beyond Tamil Nadu to the wider world.

  • International programs
  • Multi-language teaching
  • Global retreats
  • Partner organizations
06

Infrastructure Development

Building sustainable infrastructure for the long-term growth of the foundation.

  • Ashram & retreat center
  • Digital infrastructure
  • Training facilities
  • Resource center

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1
2024-2025: Foundation & Growth

Focus: Strengthen existing programs in Arcot, Chennai, and Trichy. Launch online courses. Build digital presence. Establish local satsang groups.

Key Actions: Expand teaching team, create content library, develop mobile app, conduct regional workshops.

Phase 2
2025-2026: Expansion & Outreach

Focus: Open new centers in major Tamil Nadu cities. Launch international online programs. Establish Annadhanam initiatives in all centers.

Key Actions: Train new teachers, develop multi-language materials, conduct international retreats, partner with spiritual organizations.

Phase 3
2026-2028: Global Presence

Focus: Establish presence in 5+ countries. Build permanent ashram facilities. Create a comprehensive academic curriculum for Siddhar studies.

Key Actions: Build ashram campus, publish books on Siddhar wisdom, create certification programs, host global spiritual conferences.

Phase 4
2028-2030: Sustainable Legacy

Focus: Ensure the long-term sustainability of the foundation. Establish endowments and trusts. Create a living tradition for future generations.

Key Actions: Establish Gurukul system, create endowment funds, develop research center, produce documentaries and films.
