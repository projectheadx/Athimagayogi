Thavasilar Thiru Athimagayogi

Founder & Spiritual Master, Aga Oli Meignana Yogam

Gurunathar is a realized spiritual master from the ancient Tamil Siddhar lineage. His life's mission is to bring the secret teachings of the Siddhars to humanity, making the profound science of breath and consciousness accessible to all.

With over 40 years of dedicated practice and teaching, Gurunathar has guided thousands of seekers across Tamil Nadu and beyond. His teachings blend the ancient wisdom of Vasi Yoga, breath science, and inner light meditation with practical techniques for modern life.

Gurunathar's approach is deeply compassionate and inclusive. He believes that true spiritual transformation is not reserved for monks and ascetics but is the birthright of every human being, regardless of background or circumstance.

Gurunathar's Spiritual Journey

1954
Birth & Early Life

Born in a small village near Arcot, Tamil Nadu, Gurunathar was drawn to spirituality from a very young age. His early experiences with divine visions set him on a path of deep inquiry.

1970
Meeting the Siddhar Master

At the age of 16, Gurunathar met his spiritual master, who initiated him into the secret Vasi Yoga practices. He spent the next 12 years in intense sadhana under his master's guidance.

1982
Self-Realization & Divine Vision

After years of rigorous practice, Gurunathar experienced the ultimate realization of inner light (Aga Oli). He received the divine vision and mission to spread these teachings to the world.

1985
First Public Teaching

Gurunathar began teaching in his hometown of Arcot, starting with small groups of sincere seekers. Word spread quickly about the transformative power of his teachings.

2000
Foundation of Aga Oli Meignana Yogam

Formally established the Aga Oli Meignana Yogam as a spiritual organization to systematically teach the Siddhar secrets to seekers from all walks of life.

2024 - Present
Global Reach & Digital Presence

Today, Gurunathar's teachings have reached thousands through online courses, video content, and live programs across Tamil Nadu, with plans for international expansion.
